Simple process
From surplus to served
in minutes

No complicated logistics. Post, claim, and pick up — that's the whole flow.

01
📋
Donor posts a listing

Wedding hall, canteen manager, or event organiser fills a quick form — food type, quantity, and pickup window.

02
🔔
NGOs get alerted instantly

Nearby NGOs and SHGs see the listing appear in real time and receive an immediate notification.

03
✅
NGO claims the food

One click to claim. The donor is notified with the NGO's contact details for pickup coordination.

04
🤝
Food reaches people

The NGO picks up the food and serves it. Every meal is counted toward your impact dashboard.
